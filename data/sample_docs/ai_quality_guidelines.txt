AI quality guidelines focus on accuracy, relevance, safety, traceability, and clear source grounding.

A RAG pipeline helps reduce hallucinations by retrieving approved internal context before preparing an answer. The system should avoid guessing when the answer is not available in the retrieved context.

A good quality process checks whether the answer is supported by source documents. It also reviews response accuracy, missed retrieval cases, user feedback, and repeated failure patterns.

The final goal is to make AI answers more trustworthy for enterprise users.
